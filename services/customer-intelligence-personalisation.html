Our Service

Our Customer Intelligence Platform service provides the engineering expertise to help you understand your customers on a deeper level. We design and build the systems that unify demographic, behavioural, and transactional data to predict future actions and deliver tailored experiences. By engineering the data pipelines that integrate every touchpoint—from web analytics to CRM data—we enable you to build a complete 360-degree view of your customer. This foundational platform empowers your teams to move beyond generic marketing and actively improve retention, boost conversion rates, and maximise lifetime value.

Our Approach

Our service is built around engineering a powerful Customer 360 database to act as the single source of truth for all customer information. We establish real-time data integration pipelines, using stream processors and batch ingestion to unify data from your CRM, web analytics, mobile apps, and more. Once unified, we build the AI modelling layer that allows your teams to apply advanced clustering algorithms to intelligently segment your audience and deep learning models to power a sophisticated recommendation engine. We then serve these insights via high-speed APIs, enabling your website or app to deliver personalised offers in milliseconds. The entire system is built on a continuous feedback loop, ensuring models can be dynamically updated based on the latest user interactions.

Key Platform Components

Real-Time Data Unification

We build pipelines that integrate streaming (e.g., Kafka) and batch data from CRMs, web analytics, and POS systems.

Customer 360 Database

AI-Driven Segmentation Environments

We provide the infrastructure for your teams to employ clustering algorithms (e.g., K-means) to identify valuable customer segments.

Deep Learning Recommendation Engines

We build the systems that use advanced models to predict behaviour and power personalisation.

Instant API Serving Layer

We deploy a high-speed API layer to deliver real-time recommendations and insights to your customer-facing applications.
