Our Service

Our Risk Detection & Fraud Prevention service provides the engineering expertise to build scalable, real-time intelligence systems that protect your assets and customers. We design and implement platforms that leverage sophisticated machine learning models to analyse vast streams of transactional and behavioural data, identifying suspicious patterns that signal fraud or financial risk. Our service enables you to move beyond simple rule-based alerts, empowering your teams to detect novel threats and reduce false positives. We build the infrastructure that strengthens trust, ensures compliance, and enables your business to operate with confidence.

Client Challenges

  • ✓ Increasingly sophisticated fraud schemes that evade traditional, rule-based detection systems.
  • ✓ High rates of false positives from legacy systems, which create friction for legitimate customers.
  • ✓ The challenge of building the infrastructure to monitor millions of transactions in real time to prevent financial loss.
  • ✓ Pressure to maintain strict regulatory compliance for data and transactions.

Our Approach

Our service ingests a wide array of data inputs, including transaction logs, user behaviour data, device metadata, and third-party risk feeds. We build a dedicated feature engineering layer to create rich profiles for each user, deriving time-based and relationship-based features to provide deep context for every event. We then establish an advanced modeling layer where your teams can analyse these profiles using powerful ensemble models or graph neural networks to uncover subtle, suspicious patterns. We deploy these models via a real-time scoring engine with high-throughput APIs, instantly flagging anomalies as they occur. Alerts can be automatically prioritised by severity and integrated directly into your existing alert management or SIEM systems.
